# SayHanzi 🇨🇳 — Free Chinese Learning Tools

**https://sayhanzi.com**

SayHanzi is a collection of free, no-signup tools for learning Mandarin Chinese. No ads, no paywalls — just pick a tool and start.

## What You Can Do

- 🔤 **Get Your Chinese Name** — Transliterate your English name into Chinese with meaning and pronunciation
- 🔢 **Count in Mandarin** — Learn numbers 0–100 with tap-to-hear audio, plus a listening quiz
- 🀄️ **HSK 1 Flashcards** — Spaced-repetition (SRS) flashcard system for 150 core vocabulary words
- 🐉 **Chinese Zodiac** — Find your zodiac sign by birth year and explore compatibility
- 🎵 **Tone Quiz** — Train your ear to distinguish the four Mandarin tones
- 🗣️ **Essential Phrases** — 40+ everyday Chinese phrases with audio pronunciation
- 📊 **Pinyin Chart** — Complete pinyin syllable chart with all tone combinations

## Why SayHanzi?

Most Chinese learning apps require signup, have ads, or lock features behind paywalls. SayHanzi is different:

- ✅ **100% free** — No paid plans, no premium features
- ✅ **No signup** — Start instantly, nothing to install
- ✅ **No ads** — Clean, focused learning experience
- ✅ **All tools work offline** — PWA-ready, works without internet

## Tech Stack

- Static HTML/CSS/JS
- Hosted on Cloudflare Pages
- Web Speech API for Chinese TTS
- SRS algorithm (Leitner box system) for flashcards
